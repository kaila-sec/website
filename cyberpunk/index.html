Kaila: Secure Communication

Experience secure messaging, audio/video telephony, and data exchange with Kaila. We offer more than just communication.

Why Kaila?

You want the data sovereignty to be with the users and not dependent on a cloud provider. Kaila is a decentralized peer-to-peer communication platform without central data storage.

Features

With Kaila, you can hold virtual meetings, share your screen, send messages, transfer files, and more. All always encrypted, of course.

Principles

At Kaila, we strongly believe that your data belongs to you. Therefore, it is our concern to remain true to the following principles in the development of Kaila: End-to-End Encryption, Data Sovereignty, Security by design, Device freedom, Lightweight, Easy to integrate, Autonomous, Open standards.
